Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is it?

The hackathon is from 10 A.M. to 10 P.M. on Saturday May 23th in the Packard Atrium. Check in will open at 9 A.M.

Who can attend?

Any and all current Stanford University students (SUID required).

What do I need to bring?

Bring your SUID, computer, chargers, and (maybe) a sleeping bag. We and our sponsors will be providing some hardware, but you’re welcome to bring your own hardware to hack on.

What if I don’t have experience?

Not a problem! We will be holding workshops before and at the event, and mentors will be available and eager to help out during the hackathon.

What can I build?

Anything you want! The crazier the IoT idea, the better.

Can I work in a team?

Yes! You can work in teams of up to four people.
